Overview

Executive
Summary

This is an open-ended partnership that begins with 6 focused months — and continues for as long as both sides are building something worth continuing. building Ausculta's commercial and technical foundations — not chasing leads, but making sure the product is ready, the story is sharp, the demo is irresistible, and the right structure is in place to grow fast.

02
What This Is

The
Agreement

This is not a rigid contract — it is a working partnership built on trust and shared ambition. We review together at every milestone and adapt as needed. There is no fixed end date — this runs for as long as both sides want to keep building.

What is in scope
  • Product definition — know exactly what Ausculta sells, by world, by tier, at every price point
  • Demo mastery — a demo so good that prospects ask "when can we start?" before it ends
  • Tech collaboration — active support to the engineering team to complete Stage 1 on time
  • Vovalytics integration — all capabilities fully mapped and branded under Ausculta
  • Skinny SKU — lightweight, fast-to-deploy version for clients who want to try first
  • Freelancer network — a bench of 4–6 vetted specialists ready to plug in at scale
  • Global foundation — GTM playbook and international brief delivered by end of Month 6
Explicitly NOT in scope (Months 1–3)
  • Cold lead generation — outbound pipelines come after the product story is locked
  • CRM setup — introduced later in 2026 when deal volume warrants the infrastructure
  • Mass marketing spend — no budget waste before positioning is validated

The goal of Months 1–3 is certainty: certain what Ausculta is, certain it works,

certain that a client who sees the demo wants to buy it.

03
Knowing What We Sell

Product
Clarity

Vovalytics → Ausculta

Ausculta is built on Vovalytics. The first task is to map every capability to the four Ausculta worlds and ensure the full technology is surfaced under the Ausculta brand with no orphaned features.

  • Feature audit: every Vovalytics capability documented, categorised, and assigned to a world
  • Branding gap analysis: identify any UI, API, or documentation still referencing Vovalytics
  • Priority matrix: Stage 1 must-haves vs. Phase 2 nice-to-haves
  • Client-facing feature list: plain English, benefit-led, by world — no technical jargon
The Skinny SKU — Ausculta Lite

A lightweight, fast-to-run version for clients who want to try before committing. Not a stripped-down product — a focused experience that demonstrates the most compelling value in the least time.

  • Scope: 1 world, 1 use case, 5 calls analysed — full AI output, zero integration required
  • Deployment: upload calls, receive report within 24 hours, no setup on the client side
  • Link to Try feature: feeds directly into the "Try 5 Calls Free" experience on the platform
04
The Most Important Asset

The
Demo

A great demo does not show features — it shows transformation. The prospect should leave feeling they have already seen their future with Ausculta, and that going back would be a step backwards.

Demo Architecture
  • Master demo: 20-minute journey through all four worlds with a coherent narrative
  • World-specific demos: 8-minute focused version per world for targeted conversations
  • Executive demo: 10-minute C-suite version focused on ROI, risk, and competitive positioning
  • Skinny demo: 5-minute "taste" that ends with the offer to try 5 real calls immediately
Demo Principles
Show revenue
Every feature connects to a number — win rate, resolution time, compliance cost, ROI.
Real language
Prospects hear their own words back: "objection handling", "audit trail", "CSAT forecast".
Make it personal
Where possible, use the prospect's own industry data or a relevant case simulation.
End with urgency
Close every demo with the skinny SKU offer — "let's run your last 5 calls right now."
